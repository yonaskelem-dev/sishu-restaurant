Our Story

A Legacy of
Ethiopian Taste

Born from a deep love of Ethiopian culinary tradition, Sishu is more than a restaurant — it is a living memory of flavors passed down through generations. Every dish we serve carries a story: of grandmothers and spice markets, of communal injera tables and the warmth of family.

Our chefs travel to Gondar, Tigray, and the Omo Valley to source the finest berbere, niter kibbeh, and freshly stone-milled teff — bringing the true soul of Ethiopian cuisine to every plate.
